Benefits of joining the CEI Knowledge Network include:

  • News updates on research projects, events, publications and opportunities
  • Joining open and collaborative research projects and funding bids
  • Contributing to research studies and publications
  • Hosting or participating in events, conferences, study visits, staff & student exchanges
  • PhD study opportunities
  • Open Access to our research publications and Data research portal
