Centre for Enterprise & Innovation - Open Knowledge Network

Open-ent.com houses the CEI Open Knowledge Network - our OKN. This has two sections:

Open Resources

The Open Resources portal provides access to a growing collection of freely available research and learning resources for Open Entrepreneurship. These are grouped under themes which make it easy to browse or search to find the most relevant links. They include entrepreneurship, innovation, social enterprise & innovation, education & learning, economic and sustainable development.

You can find Open data, freely available open access publications, learning resources and much more. Anyone can use the portal.

Links to search, analytics and visualisation tools will be added to aid use of the portal resources.

Research Forum

The Research Forum is where CEI members – researchers based at De Montfort University – interact with co-researchers and partner organisations worldwide. The discussion threads centre on using the OKN resources to develop research projects, innovations and practical initiatives to progress our goal of transformative social value through entrepreneurship and innovation.

Forum members will receive a regular CEI newsletter.
